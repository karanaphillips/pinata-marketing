Our Culture & Values

The principles that guide everything we do

01

Innovation First

We encourage bold ideas, experimentation, and calculated risks. Every team member is empowered to contribute innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible with AI.

02

Customer Obsession

Our customers' success is our success. We build AI agents that genuinely improve businesses and make people's work more meaningful and productive.

03

Transparency & Trust

Open communication, honest feedback, and complete transparency in our decision-making processes. We believe trust is the foundation of great teamwork.

04

Continuous Learning

The AI field evolves rapidly, and so do we. We invest heavily in learning and development, encouraging everyone to stay at the forefront of technology.

05

Ethical AI

We're committed to developing AI that augments human capabilities rather than replacing people. Our technology empowers workers and creates new opportunities.

06

Diversity & Inclusion

We believe diverse perspectives make better AI. Our team represents different backgrounds, experiences, and ways of thinking that strengthen our products.

Our Hiring Process

We've designed a thorough yet efficient process to find the best talent

1

Application Review

We carefully review your application, resume, and any work samples you provide. We aim to respond within 3-5 business days.

3-5 days
2

Phone/Video Screen

A brief conversation with our recruiting team to discuss your background, interests, and learn more about the role and Pinata.

30 minutes
3

Technical Interview

Role-specific technical assessment. For engineering roles, this includes coding challenges. For other roles, relevant skill demonstrations.

60-90 minutes
4

Team Interviews

Meet with potential teammates and managers to discuss collaboration, culture fit, and dive deeper into your experience.

2-3 hours
5

Final Round

Meet with senior leadership to discuss your vision, career goals, and how you'll contribute to Pinata's mission and growth.

45 minutes
6

Offer & Onboarding

We'll make a competitive offer and work with you to plan your successful onboarding and integration into the team.

1-2 days
