Our Story

From startup to unicorn: The journey of revolutionizing business operations

2023

The Beginning

Founded by former Google and Microsoft executives who experienced firsthand the inefficiencies of traditional business operations. The idea: what if every small business could have the same AI capabilities as tech giants?

2024

First AI Agents

Launched our first three AI agents - Executive Assistant, Social Media Manager, and Sales Associate. Early adopters reported 300-400% productivity increases within 30 days.

2024

Series A Success

Raised $15M Series A led by Sequoia Capital. Expanded team to 50+ engineers and launched Legal Assistant and Content Creator agents.

2025

QNDMBS™ Breakthrough

Developed revolutionary patent-pending QNDMBS™ technology for ethical digital marketing. Filed multiple patents and began beta testing with select partners.

2025

Series B Funding

Secured $50M Series B led by Andreessen Horowitz. Valuation reached $500M, officially achieving unicorn status.
