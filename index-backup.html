Support & Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions and access comprehensive support resources

How quickly can I see results with Pinata's AI agents?

Most businesses see immediate improvements in efficiency within 24-48 hours of implementation. Significant ROI typically becomes apparent within the first month, with some clients reporting 200-400% increases in productivity within 90 days.

Do I need to lay off my existing team?

Not at all. Many clients use Pinata to augment their existing teams, allowing human employees to focus on higher-value strategic work while AI handles routine tasks. You can scale gradually and redeploy human talent to growth areas.

What industries benefit most from Pinata?

Pinata works across all industries, but we see exceptional results in professional services, e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, healthcare, legal, and consulting. Any business with repetitive operational tasks benefits significantly.

Is there a minimum contract period?

No contracts required. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We offer a 10% discount for annual commitments, but month-to-month is available for maximum flexibility.

How do AI agents integrate with my existing systems?

Our AI agents integrate seamlessly with 500+ popular business tools including CRM systems, email platforms, social media, accounting software, and project management tools. We provide API access and custom integrations as needed.

What happens if an AI agent makes a mistake?

All AI agents include human oversight protocols and approval workflows for critical tasks. We maintain 99.9% accuracy rates and provide error insurance for the rare cases where issues occur.

Can I customize the AI agents' behavior?

Absolutely. Each agent learns your brand voice, preferences, and specific workflows. You can set custom rules, approval processes, and behavioral parameters to match your exact business requirements.

Are there any hidden fees or setup costs?

No hidden fees. The monthly subscription includes everything: AI agents, integrations, support, updates, and unlimited usage. Setup is included at no additional cost.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes, you can change plans anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. No penalties for changes.

How long does implementation take?

Basic setup takes 1-2 hours. Full integration with all your systems typically completes within 24-48 hours. Our implementation team handles everything, requiring minimal time from your staff.

How secure is my business data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and zero-trust security architecture. Your data never leaves our secure cloud environment and is protected by the same standards used by Fortune 500 companies.
